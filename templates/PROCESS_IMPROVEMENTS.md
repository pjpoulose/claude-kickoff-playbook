1. Claude or human identifies a better way to do something

3. Human reviews and approves or rejects at session end
4. Approved improvements are submitted to the canonical playbook
5. Once merged into the playbook, all future projects benefit

**The human must approve every improvement before it enters the canonical playbook.**
